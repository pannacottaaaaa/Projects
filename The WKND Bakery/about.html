2020 is the year where all the businesses are forced to close their
shops because of an unexpected pandemic that takes our world's
freedom. Many people are struggling to earn money as there is a
so-called "quarantine" that most people aren't allowed to leave their designated home.

However, there's an increase of ecommerce after the country puts into
a minimal quarantine. With that reasonable announcement, we decided to
make an online food business that most people haven't tasted the
delicacies before and what they craved in the past heavy quarantine.

The first business that we implemented was the daily buns and the
main theme of it was korean street food bread that many people doesn't
know nor tasted it for all these years. We may have a lot of complicated
issues along the process and competitions, gladly that the business
turns out very successful than we expected.

After months of being an active business, we suddenly thought about
changing the store's theme and name because making a homemade
korean street food bread takes a lot of hours to make as it needs
kneading and proofing before making into the final step of process. The
new brand name of our business was 'the WKND bakery', as it is the
website that you're seeing right now. Our products is still homemade,
but the process wasn't similar to the past products that we have. This the
start of something new to us, and we are glad that the business is doing
well up until now.

Below this page, you can see the enumerated products that we
usually did on both business implemented in these past years. While
the reviews was on our instagram story highlights.

the daily buns

how did it start?

14th of August 2020, Thursday Morning. That day was the official launch of our first business entitled "the daily buns" and we sell the famous korean bread street food called as the "cream cheese garlic bread." Our products successfully sells a lot to the point that we reached to Manila.

However, after a few months,
we changed to another business.

the WKND bakery

how did it start?

1st of March 2021, we officially re-launch our business but this time it is desserts and the new brand name was "the WKND bakery," wherein we deliver the delicacies every weekends only for you to enjoy your days. Our products are all desserts, but we have a savory pastry that is inspired to our 1st business.

So this is the WKND Bakery,
and claim your desserts in this upcoming weekends!
