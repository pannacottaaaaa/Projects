Craving for desserts but don't have it for these upcoming weekends? Got you right there!
The WKND Bakery is providing you some sweet and decent desserts that will satisfy your
stomachs even more. To be specific, all the products has only four (4) main ingredients
for each of it, and that includes the butter, flour, eggs, and one (1) specialty that
will based or in short, related on the specified dessert. Furthermore, the descriptions
for each of the products are on the images itself as they were posters. So, what are you
waiting for? Scroll down in this page, choose whatever you want for these weekends, and
click the 'online shop' if you want a faster order or the 'fill-up form' to further add
message for some important tips that you want for your orders. Please take note that if
you have allergies through the dessert/s, don't hesitate to give us a message before
placing your orders.
