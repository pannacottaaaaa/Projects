Our Story

know about the business

2020 is the year where all the businesses are forced to close their shops
because of an unexpected pandemic that takes our world's freedom. Many
people are struggling to earn money as there is a so-called "quarantine"
that most people aren't allowed to leave their designated home.

However, there's an increase of ecommerce after the country puts into a
minimal quarantine. With that reasonable announcement, we decided to make
an online food business that most people haven't tasted the delicacies
before and what they craved in the past heavy quarantine.

In the navigation bar, you could click the 'about' button to further know
our story on how we build up our first business, and helped us to rename
or inshort, renew our business.

Craving?

dessert delicacies are on your weekends!

Seeing these series of photos at your left side screen makes you crave
some after eating a salty pastries, and your whole meal won't be complete
without it. Furthermore, your weekends is your freetime as that is the time
to have desserts while doing your enjoyable activities at home.

As per usual, you can click the 'menu' button to explore our products and
know them one by one. However, if you wish to have the delicious desserts
at your home, you can click the 'Order' and see the 'fill-up form'
to write your orders or questions about the products. There is an another
option, which is by clicking the 'order basket app' under the same button
to directly order as soon as possible and receive it to us immediately.
